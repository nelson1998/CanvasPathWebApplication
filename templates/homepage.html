Welcome to CanvasPath!

CanvasPath is a course management and enrollment system that provides students and faculty members a new and interactive way to keep track of their academic tasks to better drive instruction and learning.

What can you do
with CanvasPath?

CanvasPath has many functionalities that would serve your academic needs. See below for a detailed description of each functionalities.

User Access

All users have the ability to access one out of the three possible user access - Admin, Faculty and Student. All three users have various functionalities and different content that are viewable only to that specific user.

Assignment Creation

Faculty members, within the Faculty user access, can create assignments inside the section he is teaching. The assignments include homework and exams. The assignments will viewable by the students after the faculty members create them.

Submitting Scores

Faculty member can enter the scores of an assignment for all students after grading. A grades page for each assignment would include a list of the studentâ€™s IDs and grades. The scores will be viewable by the students after grading.

Course Information

Students, within the student access system, can get information of the courses he is taking, which includes the course description, professor contact information (email and office) and course grades courses.

Organizing Teams

Users within the faculty member access can create a list of teams only for Capstone project. The user collects the information of all teams (such as team members) and he/she can maintain the team list and the members of each team.

Admin Support

Users within the admin access can manage the courses (add/remove), assign professors to courses, and enroll students to courses and sections.
